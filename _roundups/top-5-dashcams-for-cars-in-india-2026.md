## FAQs

**Which dash cam is best for Indian roads and heat conditions?**
Cameras that use a supercapacitor instead of a lithium battery, like the Qubo Dashcam Pro X and the DDPAI/PRO, are built to handle high temperatures without battery swelling issues, which matters a lot for cars parked in the Indian sun. The Qubo is specifically rated from -20°C to 85°C.

**Is 4K resolution actually better than 2K/1080p for dashcams?**
4K gives you sharper footage and better license plate readability, which the REDTIGER F7N Elite and 70mai A810 both offer on their front cameras. But if your main goal is capturing accidents and disputes clearly, 2K or 3K with a good sensor, like the STARVIS 2 in the 70mai A510, is often enough.

**Should I choose a capacitor-based or battery-based dashcam for Indian summers?**
A supercapacitor-based dashcam is generally a safer bet for Indian summers, since it's rated to handle much higher temperatures than a standard lithium battery without degrading. Both the Qubo Dashcam Pro X and DDPAI/PRO use this design.

**Do I need a rear camera in addition to a front camera?**
A rear camera is useful if you want to record what happens behind you too, such as tailgating or rear-end collisions. The dual-channel cameras in this list, the 70mai A510, REDTIGER F7N Elite, and 70mai A810, all include a front and rear setup. The Qubo and DDPAI in this list are single-channel front-only cameras.

**What is parking mode and why does it matter?**
Parking mode lets your dashcam keep recording, or start recording on motion or impact, even when the car is parked and the engine is off. On the 70mai A510 and 70mai A810, this is an optional feature that needs a separate hardwire kit. The REDTIGER F7N Elite has a built-in 24-hour parking mode.

**How much should I spend on a good dashcam in India?**
Based on the verified prices in this list, a solid dashcam starts around Rs 2,699 (DDPAI/PRO) if you just need basic front recording, and goes up to Rs 19,998 (70mai A810) if you want the best resolution, sensor, and field of view available.

**Which dashcam has the best warranty?**
The REDTIGER F7N Elite and DDPAI/PRO both come with an 18-month warranty, the longest confirmed warranty period in this list. The 70mai A510 and 70mai A810 both offer 1-year warranties, and the Qubo Dashcam Pro X's warranty is not confirmed.
